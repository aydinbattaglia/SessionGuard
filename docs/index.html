FAQ
Common questions
Does SessionGuard store my passwords or credentials?

No. SessionGuard never stores passwords or login credentials of any kind. It replays small, already-authenticated network requests that your browser has already made, using cookies and tokens that are already present in your browser's session.

Will my institution know I'm using SessionGuard?

SessionGuard sends the same keepalive requests your browser would send during normal activity. From the platform's perspective, it looks like you are simply staying active. It does not circumvent authentication or access controls.

Which platforms are supported?

Free plan: Westlaw. Pro plan: Westlaw, LexisNexis, Bloomberg Law, and PACER (pacer.gov and all uscourts.gov sub-domains).

How do I activate Pro after subscribing?

After subscribing, open the SessionGuard extension popup, click "Already subscribed? Activate →", enter the email address you used at checkout, and click Activate. Your Pro tier unlocks immediately.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing portal at any time. Your Pro access remains active until the end of the current billing period.
