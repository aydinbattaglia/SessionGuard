Frequently asked questions
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through Stripe. All payments are securely processed by Stripe — we never see your card details.

How do I activate Pro after subscribing?

After completing checkout, open the SessionGuard extension in Chrome, click "Already subscribed? Activate →", enter the email address you used at checkout, and click Activate. Pro unlocks immediately.

Can I switch between monthly and annual?

Yes. You can change your billing frequency at any time from the billing portal. Changes take effect at the next renewal.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day refund if SessionGuard isn't working for you. Email support@sessionguard.io and we'll process it, no questions asked.

Do you offer billing for law firms or teams?

Yes. Email support@sessionguard.io for volume pricing for teams of 3 or more.
