How it works

Set it once. Never think
about it again.

Install SessionGuard and log in to your platform. Everything else is automatic.

Step 01
Auto-detect
SessionGuard watches your network traffic to find the exact keepalive endpoint your platform uses. No configuration, no setup.
Step 02
Keepalive
Every few minutes, SessionGuard silently replays a small authenticated request — just enough to reset the inactivity timer.
Step 03
Stay protected
Your session stays alive indefinitely. Your credentials never leave the browser. Research uninterrupted, all day long.
